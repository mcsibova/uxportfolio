About Me

I was born and raised in Slovakia, studied aesthetics and German language at Comenius University in Bratislava, and a few years later I was professionally qualified in management with a Bachelor's degree. After many years working as a sales administrator I've chosen to make a U-turn in my career path and switch my avocation into profession. My passion for art led me to the design field. Come and take a moment to review my portfolio!

UX Design

In the accelerated transformation of digital world I would take every opportunity to help shape the online space that surrounds us.

UX Research

Using a variety of research methods I identify the commonalities across the target audience members, recognize their needs, goals, mental models.

Photo Manipulation

In order to create unique graphic design it's inevitable to work with unrepeated resources. As an amateur photographer I often use my own photographs in graphic design.
